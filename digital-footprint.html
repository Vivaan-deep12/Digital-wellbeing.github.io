Your Digital Footprint

Every action online leaves a trail. Learn the difference between active and passive footprints — and why they matter.

What is a digital footprint?

A digital footprint is the trail of information left behind whenever you use the internet — some you create on purpose, some happens automatically.

Active footprint

Created when you intentionally interact online.

  • Posting content or stories
  • Uploading photos and videos
  • Leaving comments
  • Liking and sharing posts

Passive footprint

Created automatically — often without you noticing.

  • Websites tracking your visits
  • Apps collecting usage data
  • Cookies monitoring browsing
  • Location services running quietly

Why footprints matter

  • They can stay online for years
  • Schools and employers can see them
  • They affect future opportunities
  • They can reveal personal information
