Topic 07

Gaming Safety

Online gaming is fun — and full of strangers, scams, and traps. Play safely.

Common gaming risks

  • In-game scams and fake giveaways
  • Cyberbullying in voice and text chat
  • Strangers asking for personal info
  • Account theft and password phishing

Stay safe while gaming

🔐

Strong passwords + 2FA

Every account, every time.

🙅

No personal info

Don’t share school, address or photos.

🚩

Report & block

Use the built-in tools — they work.

In-game purchases

Stick to official stores, ask a trusted adult before buying, and never log in via random "free skin" links. If a deal feels too good to be true — it almost always is.
