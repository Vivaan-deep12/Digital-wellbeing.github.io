Topic 04

Privacy & Security

Lock down your accounts, your data, and your daily habits.

What counts as personal info?

If it could identify or locate you, treat it as sensitive.

Full nameAddressSchool PhoneEmailPasswords LocationDate of birth

Strong password rules

  • 12+ characters
  • Mix of upper and lower case
  • Numbers and symbols
  • Unique per account (use a password manager)
  • Don’t use names or birthdays
  • Avoid common words like "password" or "1234"
  • Never reuse the same password
  • Don’t share passwords — ever

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA adds a second step — like a code on your phone — so even if someone guesses your password, they can’t log in. Turn it on for email, banking, social, and gaming.

Privacy settings

Review settings every few months. Limit who sees your posts, hide your location, and disable apps you no longer use.
