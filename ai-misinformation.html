AI & Misinformation

AI can create amazing things — and incredibly convincing fakes. Learn to think critically.

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is technology that performs tasks that usually require human intelligence.

ChatbotsRecommendationsVoice assistantsImage generators

Misinformation & deepfakes

Misinformation is false or misleading information shared online. Deepfakes use AI to create realistic fake images, videos and audio. They’re getting harder to spot — which is why critical thinking matters more than ever.

How to verify information

  • Check multiple sources
  • Verify the author and outlet
  • Look for evidence and citations
  • Check the publication date
  • Use trusted news websites
  • Be sceptical of viral, emotional posts
