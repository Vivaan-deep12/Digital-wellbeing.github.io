Topic 01

Your Digital Identity

A digital identity is the online version of who you are — built from every account, post, and click you make.

What is a digital identity?

A digital identity is the online version of who you are — created through everything you do on the internet.

  • Social media profiles, posts, photos, videos
  • Comments, likes, and shares
  • Online gaming accounts and search history
  • Online purchases, school and work accounts

Why does it matter?

Your digital identity can shape major parts of your life.

🎓

Education

Universities and scholarships often review your online presence.

💼

Employment

Recruiters look you up before — and after — they hire.

🤝

Relationships

People form opinions based on what they find about you.

⭐

Reputation

One bad post can echo for years.

Positive identity

  • Respectful communication
  • Academic and personal achievements
  • Helpful content and ideas
  • Responsible online behaviour

Negative identity

  • Cyberbullying
  • Offensive comments
  • Inappropriate content
  • Spreading misinformation
