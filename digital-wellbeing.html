Topic 05

Digital Wellbeing

A healthy, balanced relationship with technology supports your mind and your body.

What is digital wellbeing?

Digital wellbeing means using technology in a way that supports — rather than drains — your mental, physical, and social health.

Signs of good wellbeing

  • Balanced screen time
  • Healthy sleep habits
  • Regular physical activity
  • Positive social interactions
  • Regular breaks from screens

Risks of too much screen time

  • Sleep disruption
  • Eye strain
  • Reduced concentration
  • Stress and anxiety

Habits that help

⏸

Take breaks

Every 30–60 minutes, look away and move.

🔕

Limit notifications

Silence the noise; check on your schedule.

🌙

No screens before bed

Protects sleep quality and mood.

🌳

Get outside

Nature resets attention and stress.

👨‍👩‍👧

Real connection

Friends and family in person, not just feed.

🎯

Intentional use

Open apps with a purpose, close when done.
