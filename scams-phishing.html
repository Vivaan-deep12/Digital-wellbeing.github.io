Topic 06

Scams & Phishing

Phishing is when criminals pretend to be trusted people or brands to steal your info. Learn to spot the bait.

What is phishing?

Phishing is when criminals pose as trusted organisations or people to trick you into sharing passwords, money, or personal info.

Warning signs

  • Urgent messages ("act in 24 hours!")
  • Requests for passwords or codes
  • Suspicious links or weird domains
  • Threatening language
  • Offers that seem too good to be true

How to stay safe

  • Verify the sender before acting
  • Hover and check URLs carefully
  • Never share passwords or 2FA codes
  • Think before you click
  • When unsure, ask a trusted adult or expert
  • Report phishing to the platform
