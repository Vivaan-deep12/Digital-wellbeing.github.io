Topic 03

Your Online Reputation

Online reputation is how people perceive you based on what they find about you on the internet.

What is online reputation?

Your online reputation is the impression people form from your profiles, posts, comments, photos, and the way you behave online.

Build a positive reputation

  • Think before posting
  • Be respectful in every reply
  • Share useful and authentic content
  • Protect your privacy
  • Cyberbullying
  • Harmful comments
  • Inappropriate posts
  • Spreading false information

Who is looking?

🏫

Schools

Admissions teams may search applicants online.

💼

Employers

Most recruiters check profiles before interviews.

👥

Friends & family

The people you care about see your posts too.
